Checkpoint

Iron Frontier Express

Carry a sealed army payroll across the frontier by train. Armed bandits want it, border officers will search it, corrupt lawmen will tax it, and the dead crawl out of abandoned homes after sundown.

Survival train run Bounties Upgrades Hire crew Maxim guns Night zombies

Controls

Move with WASD or arrow keys. Aim with the mouse. Number keys select hotbar slots, F/Space/click uses the selected item, L loots nearby houses, Z brakes, X throttles, and S opens the shop at stations.

Mission

Reach Fort Mercy with the cargo intact. You can stop at frontier shops and the fort quartermaster to buy supplies, stronger weapons, armor, train parts, rail workers, hired fighters, and mounted Maxim guns.

Defeat named outlaws for bounty cash. Move across the train to line up shots, watch the fuel gauge, and let crew upgrades cover you when the rail gets crowded.
